Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

VoyaLift is a fitness and nutrition tracking app. This policy explains what data the app handles, why it is used, and what choices you have.

Information you provide

You may enter profile details, body metrics, workout records, nutrition entries, water intake, notes, progress photos, custom plans, and training preferences. This information is used to provide logging, history, progress tracking, training recommendations, and related app features.

Apple Health and Apple Watch

VoyaLift may read or write Apple Health data only after you grant permission. This can include steps, workouts, body weight, sleep, heart-related metrics, water, and other wellness data supported by the app. You can change Health permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app or the Apple Health app.

Apple Watch features may sync workout events, set logs, heart-related context, and recovery-related data between the Watch app and the iPhone app. These features are optional.

Photos and AI food estimates

If you use photo-based food estimation, the selected food photo may be sent to an AI service for recognition and nutrition estimation. Results are approximate and are not saved until you confirm them in the app.

If you use text-based AI meal estimation or AI plan generation, the text you enter may be processed by an AI service to generate the requested estimate or plan. AI output is for personal fitness reference only and is not medical advice.

Local storage and exports

Workout records, nutrition records, body metrics, notes, and many app settings are stored on your device. VoyaLift lets you export or delete data from within the app where supported. Device deletion, system limits, or third-party service failures may make data unrecoverable, so keep your own backup of important records.

Diagnostics, purchases, and third-party services

The app may use anonymous diagnostics or crash logs to improve stability. Subscriptions and purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. If ads, analytics, or AI services are enabled, the related third-party service may process limited data needed to provide that feature.

Health and fitness disclaimer

VoyaLift provides workout, nutrition, weight, and wellness information for general fitness reference. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, medical advice, or professional nutrition prescriptions. Consult a qualified professional before starting exercise, changing your diet, or changing treatment.

Your choices

  • You can manage Apple Health permissions in system settings.
  • You can choose whether to use AI photo or text features.
  • You can export or delete supported records in the app.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Apple ID subscription settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@voyalift.com.
